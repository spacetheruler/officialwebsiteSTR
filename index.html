Official Biography · 2026

141 Songs. 4 Businesses. One Vision. Entirely On His Own Terms.

A 20-year-old independent rapper, producer, and creative entrepreneur. His output is staggering — over 141 songs released before his 21st birthday, six or more dropped monthly, and a growing Planetary EP series where each chapter is a world of its own.

Across YouTube, TikTok, and beyond, his catalog has accumulated hundreds of thousands of views, with individual tracks routinely crossing 100K and generating waves of organic fan-created content that extend his world far beyond official releases.

His catalog includes cornerstone projects — RnSpaceB, Y2K (Upgraded), and the centerpiece Venus (Continued) — alongside a rare simultaneous release of Spaceman, Peachdeluxe, and Crown, marking a unique multi-album expansion moment in his region.
